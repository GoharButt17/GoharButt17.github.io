History

​​​​Al-Karam International Institute is taking forward the legacy of religious Institute (DMG) which was established in 1925 to bring a much-needed revolution in the education sector. Our Institute focuses on both modern and Islamic education because it is important for the younger generation to have strong religious roots and be familiar with Islamic teachings and principles to lead successful careers and contribute to the prosperity of society. The Institute was formally chartered by the Parliament of Pakistan under the Al-Karam International Institute Act, 2021. This Institute is being established with the goal of becoming the leading degree awarding institute. Al-Karam aims to provide quality education to produce enlightened scholars for the nation’s advancement and promote the peaceful image of Islam at an international level to counter the conspiracies against Muslims and Islam. Our institute focuses on broadening the vision of students so they can develop and implement viable solutions for the betterment of the society, Country and the Muslim Ummah
